Positioning

An intelligence layer on top of what you already run — including SAP.

GRIPPX does not replace your ERP, your existing ATG hardware, or your fleet telematics. It reconciles their data into one operational picture, then writes verified stock movements, deliveries and losses back into your system of record so finance and executives keep working from the numbers they already trust.

ERP & business systems

SAP

SAP (ECC / S/4HANA)

Goods receipt and goods issue documents posted via IDoc / OData for every depot delivery and dispensing event. Stock levels reconcile to MM inventory in near real time.

Sage

Stock and invoicing sync for mid-market finance teams running Sage Evolution or Sage 300.

Microsoft Dynamics

Inventory and cost-centre postings via native connector, including cross-border cost allocation.

Oracle

Financial and supply-chain modules integrated through REST / SOAP adapters.

Power BI / Azure
