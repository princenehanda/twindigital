The problem

Fleet tracking tells you where a truck is. It doesn't tell you where the fuel went.

01

Fragmented systems

GPS tracking, depot dip readings and ERP stock records live in separate tools that don't talk to each other.

02

Unverified loss

Siphoning, loading discrepancies and delivery shortfalls are discovered in the books weeks after they happen.

03

Manual reconciliation

Opening stock, deliveries and dispensed volumes are still tied out by hand at month end.

04

No cross-border visibility

Operations spanning multiple countries lose continuity of data at every border post.

05

Reactive maintenance

Filters, pumps and tank probes are serviced on a calendar, not on measured condition.

06

Slow executive reporting

By the time a loss shows up on a dashboard, the fuel — and the margin — is already gone.

Platform modules

One data model. Every stage of the fuel lifecycle.

Module 01

Wetstock management

Live tank levels, automatic tank gauging and reconciliation against a 0.3% variance tolerance at every depot.

Module 02

Tanker intelligence

Independent monitoring of every compartment, with loading verification and en-route discharge detection.

Module 03

Truck fuel consumption

Separate telemetry for the vehicle's own diesel tank — theft alerts, idle burn, litres per kilometre.

Module 04

Bulk fuel & bowsers

Static tanks, bowsers and generators with authorised dispensing and a full audit trail of who drew fuel, when.

Module 05

Delivery verification

Electronic proof of delivery — GPS-verified signature, timestamp, photos and delivered volume against the order.

Module 06

Operational intelligence

Abnormal fuel movement, leak detection, driver risk index, predicted stock-outs and route optimisation — from the same telemetry stream, not a generic model bolted on top.

Module 07

Executive dashboards

Role-based views for CEOs, operations managers and finance — inventory, losses, revenue and fleet status in one screen.

Module 08

Mobile applications

Drivers get trips and digital paperwork; technicians get calibration and maintenance; managers get everything.

Module 09

ERP & system integration

Open integration with SAP, Sage, Microsoft Dynamics, Oracle and existing ATG hardware — no rip and replace.
